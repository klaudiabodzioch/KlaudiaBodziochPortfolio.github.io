About me

Helllo! I am Klaudia and I am a dedicated Data Analyst currently working as a consultant for Richemont. With a strong background in data analysis and a passion for ensuring data quality, I have successfully transitioned from a graduate role to a key position within my current organization. My career journey reflects my commitment to continuous learning and professional growth in the field of data analysis and engineering.

Professional Journey

My career in data began as a Graduate Data Analyst at Feefo, a company renowned for providing authenticated reviews for other businesses. During my tenure at Feefo, I engaged in extensive data analysis for sales and marketing teams, leveraging tools such as SQL (BigQuery), PowerBi, and Looker. One of my notable achievements was developing an ROI calculator for our prospective clients, which was prominently featured on our website. Additionally, I played a significant role in a migration project that required proficiency in SQL (DBeaver), dbt, Looker Studio, GitHub, and Alteryx. After 10 months at Feefo, I relocated to Switzerland to take on a new challenge as a Quality Data Engineer Intern. In this role, I expanded my skill set by gaining knowledge of Monte Carlo (a Data Quality Tool), SAP, and IQD, while continuing to utilize SQL (MySQL). My ability to adapt and integrate quickly into the team led to my transition from an intern to a permanent position. As a result, I am now the primary person responsible for the quality of data across various Maisons at Richemont.

Current Role and Responsibilities

In my current position, I am responsible for ensuring the quality of data across different Maisons at Richemont. My responsibilities include: - Setting up domains and Teams channels for Monte Carlo. - Cleaning up and managing data tables to reduce costs by removing unused or outdated tables. - Facilitating the onboarding of new Maisons to IQD, making their tables available in SAP and SQL, and providing training on how to use the tools. - Documenting processes extensively in Confluence. - Problem-solving and addressing issues reported by different Maisons, such as data visibility problems, and escalating issues when necessary.

Motivation and Future Aspirations

My primary goal is to continue developing my expertise in data analysis and engineering. I am driven by a desire to learn and master different tools and technologies in the field. Joining the World Trade Organization represents an ideal next step in my career progression. I am confident that my skills, experience, and passion for data quality will make a valuable contribution to the WTO's data initiatives.
